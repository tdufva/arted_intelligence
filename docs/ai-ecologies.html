Source Boundary

Argument

The comparison suggests that AI research often treats intelligence as measurable, benchmarked, agentic capability, while art education more often treats it as situated, creative, pedagogical, and relational. Umwelt opens a third path: intelligence as ecology.

What this means for art education

If intelligence is ecological rather than singular, then art education has a distinct role: it can teach situated perception, shared world-building, and ethical relation across human, machine, and more-than-human forms of cognition.
